## ğŸµ Animation Rhythm

Based on musical timing for natural feel:

- **Background gradient**: 3 seconds (waltz time)
- **Icon rotation**: 3 seconds (synchronized with background)
- **Icon breathing**: 2 seconds (slow breath)
- **Shimmer sweep**: 2 seconds (energetic)
- **Particle emission**: 1.5 seconds (exciting)
- **Message transition**: 0.4 seconds (quick snap)
